2025 ML · Classification

Customer Churn Prediction

Built a full ML pipeline on 10,000 bank customers — from raw data to a serialized, deployment-ready model achieving 86.8% accuracy and 0.869 ROC-AUC. Not just training a model — architecting a system you can actually plug in.

  • Benchmarked 5 classification algorithms (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, AdaBoost, SVC) using stratified cross-validation — ensuring fair, leak-free evaluation.
  • Gradient Boosting surfaced as the winner. Feature engineering was key: balance-per-product and salary-balance ratio were custom signals that boosted signal quality.
  • Preprocessing pipeline: standard scaling + one-hot encoding, all integrated into a reproducible scikit-learn workflow.
  • Serialized via joblib — ready for real-time inference on new customer records, not just notebook demos.
Python Scikit-learn Pandas NumPy Gradient Boosting Joblib

2025 Data Analytics · BI

Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

Transformed 3,900 retail transactions into a living analytics system — SQL-powered segmentation, a Power BI dashboard, and a stakeholder-ready report. The kind of output a business team actually uses.

  • Segmented 3,900 customers into Loyal (3,116), Returning (701), and New (83) using 10 purpose-built PostgreSQL queries — each one answering a specific business question.
  • Revenue breakdown sliced by gender, age group, and product category — giving business stakeholders a multi-dimensional view with minimal effort.
  • Feature engineering: age group buckets, purchase frequency in days — turning raw dates and ages into actionable analytical signals.
  • Handled missing values with category-wise median imputation. Loaded into PostgreSQL via SQLAlchemy — no manual CSV wrangling.
  • Power BI dashboard surfacing KPIs: avg. purchase value ($59.76), subscription rate (27%), and category-level revenue trends — interactive, filterable, stakeholder-ready.
Python PostgreSQL Power BI Pandas SQLAlchemy

2025 Automation · LLM · BI

Financial Analysis Automation Dashboard

Wired together n8n, Gemini 2.5 Flash, GPT-4.1-mini, and Chart.js into a zero-touch financial intelligence system. Drop in PDF reports, get out a full interactive dashboard — no human in the loop.

  • Automated extraction of revenue, gross profit, net profit, and period comparisons from multi-company PDF reports — using LLMs as structured parsers, not just chatbots.
  • Dual-model approach: Gemini 2.5 Flash + GPT-4.1-mini for cross-validation of extracted financial metrics — increasing reliability and catching model-specific errors.
  • Dynamic HTML dashboard built with Chart.js: KPI cards, revenue bar charts, radar-based growth visualization, margin trend lines, and market share doughnut charts — all auto-populated from LLM output.
  • This isn't a prototype — it's a blueprint for AI-native financial reporting at scale.
